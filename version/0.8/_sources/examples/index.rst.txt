========

files for LS-DYNA and then solve them from Python. Many are adapted from
the `LS-DYNA Knowledge Base <_ls_dyna_knowledge_base>`_.

.. _ls_dyna_knowledge_base: https://lsdyna.ansys.com/knowledge-base/
